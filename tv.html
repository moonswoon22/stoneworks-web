Audience & reach

Approximate platform figures (they shift over time).

  • YouTube — ~600 subscribers · ~95K lifetime views
  • TikTok — ~45K followers · on the order of ~700K views/year when active
  • Instagram — ~2K followers · ~30K monthly views and growing

Instagram sits around a 50/50 gender split in a similar age band to other channels. Across platforms, 50%+ of viewers are typically 25–34, with a modest skew older beyond that.

Across platforms, audience skews younger adult; TikTok leans more female, YouTube more male — long-form is a chance to widen the middle.

What this channel cluster is about

  • Society & culture: technoculture, media history, “tech isn’t neutral,” public understanding of science.
  • Inclusive / non-gatekept STEM: curiosity-first, “no assumed background,” repair literacy, women and girls in STEM — with majority-female TikTok as one signal of who already shows up.
