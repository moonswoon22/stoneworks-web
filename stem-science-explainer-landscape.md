## Executive summary

YouTube still anchors credibility and revenue. The landscape splits into **three eras**: legacy edutainment (Veritasium, Hank Green), women maker pioneers (Simone Giertz, Becky Stern), and modern spectacle builders (Mark Rober, Hacksmith at 79M).

**What changed in 2026:** short-form isn't repurposed long-form anymore. Three native lanes are winning — **Maker Girlies** (creative-tech montage, viral since Spring 2026), **greenscreen deep dives** (k4554ndr4), and **tech-reporter optimism** (Cleo Abram, ATARBYTE).

**Biggest white space:** women-led spectacle builds at Mark Rober production tier, and engineering depth inside the creative-tech aesthetic — not more aesthetic montage without substance.

## Instagram & TikTok — where format is reinventing

### Short-form creator map

| Creator | Audience | Archetype | Differentiator |
|---------|----------|-----------|----------------|
| Cleo Abram | 8M | Tech reporter | *Huge If True*; optimistic tech journalism |
| k4554ndr4 | 1.2M TT / 500K IG | Deep dive | Monotone greenscreen research essays |
| Emily the Engineer | 3M TT / 841K IG | Comedy-tech | Sarcastic maker humor; YT ≪ TT |
| Daisy Foko Granson | ~1M TT | Inventor vlog | Lifestyle STEM; explainers over builds |
| ATARBYTE | IG / TT | Tech reporter | Electronics niche; distinct internet aesthetic |
| ubeboobey · mewtru · emma orhun · meshtimes | — | Maker Girlies | Creative-tech montage wave (Spring 2026) |

### Priority trends

1. **Native short-form formats win** — not YouTube clips. Greenscreen essays, creative-tech montage, comedy-engineering each have their own IP.
2. **Aesthetic is a moat** — Maker Girlies (Y2K craft) and ATARBYTE (manic pixie electronics) prove tone and look drive discovery as much as technical depth.
3. **Women-in-STEM is bifurcating** — creative-tech and deadpan research lanes are growing; high-budget spectacle tier remains male-dominated (Mark Rober / Hacksmith archetype).

**Maker Girlies (Spring 2026):** Gen Z women blending art × tech with accessible, Y2K-influenced aesthetics. Community infrastructure growing — stained-glass workshops (LA), cyberdeck classes at TIAT.

**Deep dive lane:** k4554ndr4 — 1.2M TikTok, 500K IG. Proves information density works on short-form when the research hook is strong.

## White space — where a new show could land

1. **Women-led spectacle builds** at Mark Rober production tier — the gap is real and unfilled
2. **Creative-tech with engineering depth** — beyond aesthetic montage into real build literacy
3. **Cross-platform franchise** — TikTok-native voice → YouTube long-form (Emily the Engineer trajectory)
4. **Industry insider × creator format** — Astrosamantha lane, barely occupied
